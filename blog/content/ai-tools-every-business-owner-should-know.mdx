## AI Tools Have Changed

A year ago, AI tools were mostly novelties. Today, they're essential business tools. The difference? They've become practical, affordable, and genuinely useful for everyday tasks.

Here are the 10 AI tools we recommend to every business owner we work with.

## For Writing and Communication

### 1. Claude (by Anthropic)

**What it does:** Writes emails, proposals, reports, and marketing copy. Analyzes documents. Answers questions about your business data.

**Why we love it:** Claude is the most thoughtful AI assistant available. It gives nuanced, well-structured responses and can handle complex business documents.

**Cost:** Free tier available, Pro at $20/month

### 2. Grammarly

**What it does:** Checks grammar, tone, and clarity in everything you write — emails, documents, social media posts.

**Why we love it:** It works everywhere (browser, email, docs) and catches errors you'd miss.

**Cost:** Free tier, Business at $15/user/month

## For Design and Content

### 3. Canva (with AI features)

**What it does:** Creates social media graphics, presentations, and marketing materials with AI-assisted design.

**Why we love it:** Even non-designers can create professional visuals in minutes.

**Cost:** Free tier, Pro at $15/month

### 4. Descript

**What it does:** Video and podcast editing using AI. Edit video by editing text — delete a sentence from the transcript, and it's removed from the video.

**Why we love it:** Makes video content accessible for businesses that don't have a videographer.

**Cost:** Free tier, Business at $24/month

## For Automation and Productivity

### 5. n8n

**What it does:** Connects your business tools and automates workflows — from simple data syncs to complex multi-step processes.

**Why we love it:** Open-source, self-hostable, and incredibly powerful. It's what we use for most of our client automations.

**Cost:** Free (self-hosted), Cloud from $20/month

### 6. Notion AI

**What it does:** AI assistant built into Notion for note-taking, project management, and document creation.

**Why we love it:** If you already use Notion, the AI add-on is a no-brainer. It summarizes meetings, generates action items, and drafts content.

**Cost:** $10/member/month add-on

## For Customer Service

### 7. Intercom (with Fin AI)

**What it does:** AI-powered customer support that resolves up to 50% of inquiries automatically.

**Why we love it:** Fin actually understands context and can resolve complex questions, not just simple FAQs.

**Cost:** From $39/seat/month

## For Sales and Marketing

### 8. Apollo.io

**What it does:** AI-powered prospecting that finds leads, enriches contact data, and automates outreach sequences.

**Why we love it:** Combines lead database, email outreach, and CRM in one tool.

**Cost:** Free tier, Professional at $49/month

### 9. Jasper

**What it does:** AI content creation specifically designed for marketing teams — blog posts, ads, social media, and email campaigns.

**Why we love it:** Unlike general AI assistants, Jasper is trained specifically on marketing best practices.

**Cost:** From $49/month

## For Meetings

### 10. Otter.ai

**What it does:** Transcribes meetings in real-time, generates summaries, and identifies action items automatically.

**Why we love it:** Never take manual meeting notes again. The AI summary is ready seconds after the meeting ends.

**Cost:** Free tier, Pro at $16.99/month

## How to Get Started

Don't try to adopt all 10 tools at once. Here's our recommended order:

1. **Start with Claude or ChatGPT** — Get comfortable with AI assistants
2. **Add Grammarly** — Instant improvement in all your communication
3. **Try Canva** — Level up your visual content
4. **Explore n8n or Zapier** — Start automating your most repetitive tasks
5. **Add tools as needed** — Let your specific pain points guide your choices
