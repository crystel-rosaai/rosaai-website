## Your Website Is Your First Impression

In 2026, your website is often the first interaction a potential customer has with your business. Studies show that users form an opinion about your site in just 50 milliseconds. If that first impression is negative, they're gone — and they're not coming back.

## The 5 Most Common Website Mistakes

### 1. Slow Load Times

If your site takes more than 3 seconds to load, you're losing over half your visitors. Speed isn't just a nice-to-have — it directly impacts your revenue.

**Fix:** Optimize images, use modern frameworks like Next.js, and leverage CDNs for global delivery.

### 2. No Clear Call to Action

Visitors land on your page and... don't know what to do next. Every page should have a clear, compelling next step.

**Fix:** Add prominent CTAs above the fold. Use action-oriented language like "Book a Free Consultation" instead of "Learn More."

### 3. Not Mobile-Friendly

Over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices. If your site doesn't look great on a phone, you're ignoring the majority of your audience.

**Fix:** Use responsive design that adapts to any screen size. Test on actual devices, not just browser dev tools.

### 4. Outdated Design

Design trends evolve. A website that looked modern in 2020 can look dated today. Users associate outdated design with an outdated business.

**Fix:** Refresh your design with modern principles — clean typography, generous whitespace, subtle animations, and a consistent color palette.

### 5. No Social Proof

People trust other people. If your website doesn't show reviews, testimonials, or case studies, you're missing a powerful conversion tool.

**Fix:** Add testimonials, client logos, case study highlights, and metrics that demonstrate your impact.

## What a Modern Website Should Do

A great website in 2026 should:

- Load in under 2 seconds
- Guide visitors toward a clear action
- Look stunning on every device
- Build trust through social proof
- Reflect your brand's personality
