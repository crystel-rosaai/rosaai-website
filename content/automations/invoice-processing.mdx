An Edmonton distribution company was processing invoices by hand — printing PDFs, keying numbers into spreadsheets, and chasing down approvals over email. With dozens of vendors and hundreds of invoices a month, mistakes were common and the bottleneck was real.

RosaAI deployed an AI-powered pipeline that uses OCR to pull data from PDF and scanned invoices, then matches each one against open purchase orders automatically. When something does not line up — wrong amount, missing PO, duplicate submission — the system flags it for a human to review instead of letting it slip through. Invoices that pass validation get routed to the right approver based on dollar thresholds.

Processing time dropped by 90%, and the AP team went from spending most of their week on data entry to focusing on the exceptions that actually need their attention. The company also caught discrepancies that had been going unnoticed for months.
