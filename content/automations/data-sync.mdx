An Alberta-based retailer was running their business across four platforms — Shopify for online sales, a CRM for customer relationships, QuickBooks for accounting, and a warehouse system for inventory. Every time an order came in, someone had to update three other systems by hand. Data got stale, inventory counts drifted, and customer records contradicted each other.

RosaAI built a real-time data sync pipeline that connects all four platforms with bi-directional updates. When an order lands in Shopify, inventory adjusts everywhere. When a customer updates their address in the CRM, it flows through to shipping. The system handles conflicts automatically — if two platforms update the same record, predefined rules decide which one wins — and logs everything so nothing happens silently.

The retailer eliminated manual data entry entirely and finally has one version of the truth. Inventory discrepancies that used to cause overselling and refunds dropped to near zero, and the operations team can trust the numbers they see without cross-checking three dashboards.
