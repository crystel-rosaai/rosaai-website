A Lethbridge wellness studio was sending the same generic newsletter to their entire list every week. Open rates were low, unsubscribes were creeping up, and they had no idea which messages were actually driving bookings.

RosaAI set up behavior-triggered email sequences that send the right message at the right time. When someone signs up for a trial class, they get a welcome series tailored to their interests. When a member has not booked in two weeks, they get a gentle nudge with availability. Each email uses dynamic content blocks so the messaging feels personal without anyone writing individual emails.

Open rates jumped to 45%, and the studio saw a measurable increase in rebookings from the re-engagement sequences alone. The owner stopped spending Sunday evenings writing newsletters and let the automation handle the heavy lifting.
