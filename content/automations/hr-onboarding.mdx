A fast-growing tech company in Calgary was hiring several people a month, and the onboarding process was a mess. HR would spend two full days per new hire collecting documents, requesting IT accounts, assigning training tasks, and coordinating intro meetings — all through email chains and shared checklists that inevitably got missed.

RosaAI built an onboarding workflow that triggers the moment a new hire is confirmed in the HR system. The automation sends the new hire a secure portal to upload required documents, provisions their email and tool accounts, creates a personalized onboarding checklist with deadlines, and schedules intro meetings with their team and key stakeholders. HR gets a progress dashboard showing exactly where each new hire stands.

What used to take two days now wraps up in about two hours of actual human involvement. New hires show up on day one with everything ready — accounts active, meetings on the calendar, and a clear roadmap for their first week. HR finally has bandwidth to focus on the human side of onboarding instead of the paperwork.
