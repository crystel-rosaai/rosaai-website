A Calgary-based consulting firm was losing track of new clients between the intake form and the first meeting. Information lived in three different places, welcome emails went out late (or not at all), and the team never knew when a new client had signed on until someone mentioned it in the hallway.

RosaAI built a single n8n workflow that watches for new form submissions, creates the CRM contact with every field mapped correctly, kicks off a personalized welcome email sequence, spins up a project folder with templated tasks, and pings the team channel in Slack with the client summary. The whole thing runs without anyone touching it.

The result: the firm reclaimed over five hours a week of admin time, new clients started getting their welcome materials within minutes instead of days, and the team had full visibility from the moment someone hit "submit." No more clients falling through the cracks.
