A Calgary physiotherapy clinic was dealing with a no-show rate that was eating into revenue. The front desk was spending hours each day making reminder calls, and patients who needed to reschedule would just skip the appointment instead of calling in.

RosaAI built an end-to-end scheduling system that syncs with the clinic's calendar in real time, sends automated SMS and email reminders at 48 hours and 2 hours before each appointment, and gives patients a one-click link to reschedule if something comes up. After each visit, the system triggers a follow-up workflow with care instructions and a prompt to book the next session.

No-shows dropped by 80%, and the front desk staff got their time back for patient care instead of phone tag. The clinic also saw an increase in rebookings thanks to the automated follow-ups that patients actually appreciated.
