A growing Alberta e-commerce brand was selling across their own Shopify store, Amazon, and a couple of local retail partners. Orders came in from everywhere, and the fulfillment process was a patchwork of manual steps — checking inventory in one system, printing labels in another, and sending tracking emails by hand when they remembered.

RosaAI built a unified order pipeline that captures orders from every sales channel, updates inventory in real time so nothing gets oversold, auto-generates shipping labels with the right carrier, and sends customers tracking notifications at each stage. After delivery confirmation, the system waits a few days and sends a review request email to build up their product reviews.

The brand hit 99% on-time delivery within the first month and saw a noticeable jump in customer reviews. The founder went from spending evenings managing fulfillment to checking a dashboard once a day, and their customers started commenting on how polished the post-purchase experience felt.
