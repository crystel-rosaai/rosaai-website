A Red Deer marketing agency was spending hours each week fielding website inquiries that went nowhere. Their sales team would jump on every lead, only to find out halfway through a call that the prospect was not the right fit — wrong budget, wrong industry, or just browsing.

RosaAI built a conversational AI bot that sits on their website and handles the first conversation. It asks the qualifying questions the sales team would ask anyway — budget range, timeline, what they are looking for — scores each lead against custom criteria, and books discovery calls directly on the calendar for prospects that meet the threshold. Everyone else gets helpful resources and a polite redirect.

The agency tripled their qualified lead volume without adding headcount. Their sales team now walks into every call knowing the prospect is a real opportunity, and no one wastes time on leads that were never going to close.
