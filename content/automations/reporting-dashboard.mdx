A mid-sized Edmonton agency was spending every Monday morning pulling numbers from Google Analytics, their CRM, Meta Ads, and LinkedIn — copying data into spreadsheets, building charts, and emailing PDF reports to their clients. It took one person nearly half a day, every single week.

RosaAI automated the entire reporting pipeline. The system pulls data from all connected platforms on a schedule, aggregates it into a clean dashboard with the KPIs that actually matter, generates a branded PDF report with trend highlights, and emails it to each stakeholder automatically. If a metric swings significantly, the report calls it out so nobody has to dig for the insight.

The agency reclaimed four hours a week and their clients started getting reports that were more consistent and more useful than the hand-built versions. The team lead who used to own the Monday morning report now spends that time on strategy instead of spreadsheets.
